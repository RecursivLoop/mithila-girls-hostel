Fresh Home-cooked Meals, Four Times a Day

A rotating weekly menu prepared in-house — no outside caterers. Poori-sabzi, chhole, idli, khichdi, chicken on Sundays, chai twice a day. Menu adjusted with the seasons.

What a typical day tastes like

  • 6:30 AM — Morning chai
  • 8:30–9:30 AM — Breakfast (roti-sabzi, poori-chhole, idli, paratha…)
  • 2:00 PM — Lunch (dal, chawal, sabzi, salad, papad)
  • 6:30 PM — Evening chai + snacks (biscuit, pakora, chura-badam)
  • 8:30 PM — Dinner (roti-sabzi, mixed veg, sometimes chicken or paneer)

Menu on the wall is the actual weekly rotation — visit and see for yourself.
